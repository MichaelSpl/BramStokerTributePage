Bram Stoker

Creator of the world's most famous vampire.

Timeline of importent events involving Bram Stoker:

  • 1847 - Born in Dublin, Ireland.
  • 1870 - Finished university at Trinity College with a degree in Mathematics.
  • 1878 - Met Sir Henry Irving and became his manager.
  • 1879 - Published his first book, The Duties of Clerks of Petty Sessions in Ireland.
  • 1890 - Published his first novel, The Snake’s Pass.
  • 1897 - Published his most famous novel, Dracula.
  • 1902 - Published The Mystery of the Sea.
  • 1903 - Published The Jewel of Seven Stars.
  • 1909 - Published The Lady of the Shroud.
  • 1912 - Died in London, England.
  • 1914 - Florence Stoker, Bram’s widow, published Dracula’s Guest.
  • 2009 - Dacre Stoker, Bram’s great grandnephew, and Ian Holt produced Dracula: The Un-Dead.
